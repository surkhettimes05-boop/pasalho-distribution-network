1. Rep order entry
- The rep selects a retailer.
- The rep adds products and quantities.
- The rep saves the order as pending.

2. Warehouse view
- The warehouse sees all pending and confirmed orders.
- The warehouse can confirm or deliver orders.
- Stock is reduced when an order is saved.

3. Distributor view
- The distributor sees the full dashboard.
- The distributor can manage products, retailers, orders, inventory, and ledger.
- The distributor can also see analytics and reorder suggestions.
